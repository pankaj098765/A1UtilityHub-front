Limitations & responsible use

                    
While this generator helps you create better prompts, it has limits:

                    
    
                        
  • Quality depends on input: Short, vague topics produce limited prompts. The more context you provide, the better the output.
    • 
                        
  • Not a content guarantor: A well-formed prompt increases chances of a good response, but AI models can still hallucinate facts or produce biased text. Always verify important details before publishing or using commercially.
    • 
                        
  • Model rules and licensing: Generated prompts are for your use, but outputs from third-party AI services may have their own terms ‚Äî e.g., commercial restrictions for images. Check the terms of the AI service you use.
    • 
                    


                    
Practical tips for better prompts

                    
    
                        
  • Be specific: Mention word counts, formats (e.g., list, bullet points), tone, and any examples that illustrate your expectation.
    • 
                        
  • Use constraints: If you want short answers, set a max sentence or word limit. For images, specify aspect, style, and color palette.
    • 
                        
  • Iterate: If the output is close but not perfect, tweak the prompt to add or remove constraints rather than changing the whole idea.
