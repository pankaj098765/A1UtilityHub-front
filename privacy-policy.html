Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24/07/2025

At A1UtilityHub, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our online tools. By accessing our site, you agree to the practices described here. We encourage you to read this policy carefully so you understand how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information to improve user experience and maintain our services. The types of information include:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name and email address, when you contact us through our form or subscribe to updates.
  • Non-Personal Information: Device type, browser version, operating system, referring URLs, and general browsing behavior.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use them to remember preferences, enhance usability, and serve relevant ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect helps us to:

  • Improve the performance and reliability of our tools.
  • Personalize user experience and recommend content.
  • Analyze trends and site performance for optimization.
  • Display relevant ads through Google AdSense and other advertising partners.
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms and protect against misuse.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

A1UtilityHub uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies, known as DoubleClick DART cookies, to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website and other sites on the internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also learn more about how Google uses data by visiting the Google Advertising Policy page.

Third-party vendors and ad networks may also serve ads on our site. These vendors may collect information directly from your browser via cookies or similar technologies, subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies & User Choices

Cookies are essential for enhancing the browsing experience. They allow us to store user preferences, keep you logged in, and show more relevant results. However, you have full control:

  • You may disable cookies via your browser settings.
  • You can clear existing cookies from your browser at any time.
  • Opt-out options for Google Ads are available through official Google tools.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security practices to protect your personal information. These include firewalls, encryption where applicable, and restricted access to sensitive data. While we strive for strong security, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against all potential threats.

6. Third-Party Services

We may integrate APIs, analytics tools, or widgets from third-party service providers. These providers operate under their own privacy policies and may collect information directly through their services embedded on our site.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe that your child has provided such information, please contact us, and we will take immediate steps to remove it.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or unnecessary information.
  • Withdraw consent to data collection at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us directly at the email provided below.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in laws, technologies, or business practices. Any modifications will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services means you accept those changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out. Transparency and trust are at the core of A1UtilityHub, and we are always open to resolving your concerns.
