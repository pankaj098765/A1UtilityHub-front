Step 2: Salary Components You Should Understand

When you look at your salary slip, you‚Äôll notice multiple terms like Basic, HRA, Allowances, and Perquisites. Not all of these are fully taxable:

  • Basic Salary: Always 100% taxable.
  • HRA (House Rent Allowance): Partially exempt if you pay rent.
  • Special Allowances: Fully taxable (e.g., conveyance).
  • Leave Travel Allowance (LTA): Exempt twice in 4 years if used for travel within India.
  • Perquisites: Benefits like a company car, accommodation, or shares‚Äîtaxed based on valuation.
  • Bonus/Incentives: Fully taxable in the year received.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Forgetting to declare HRA while living on rent.
  • Not submitting proof of investments under 80C.
  • Confusing CTC with taxable salary.
  • Ignoring surcharge and cess while computing manually.
  • Not comparing old vs new regime before filing returns.
